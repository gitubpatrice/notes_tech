v1.0.8 — Hotfix for stuck startup (blank page)

Critical fixes
• Fixed startup stuck on a blank page with logo: rolled back the
  parallelization of `FlutterGemma.initialize()` introduced in v1.0.7.
  Init is now back to a strict `await` at the start of `main()`
  (proven v1.0.6 pattern). Cost: ~80-200 ms cold-start regression on
  S9 — preferable to a blocking risk.
• Removed SQLCipher PRAGMA `mmap_size = 64 MB`: on some Android native
  builds, the call could hang silently at cold boot. The
  `cache_size = 32 MB` PRAGMA is kept (FTS perf gain unchanged, no risk).

All v1.0.7 hardening still in effect (15 fixes perf/quality/security/UI):
dynamic SecureWindow, exponential PIN lockout, async MiniLM indexing
worker, atomic ONNX write, clean disposal, R8 full mode, etc.

flutter analyze: 0 errors, 0 warnings. flutter test: 64/64 passed.
